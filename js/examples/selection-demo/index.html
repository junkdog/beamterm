How to Use:

  • Mouse Selection: Click and drag to select text
  • Auto Copy: Selected text is automatically copied to clipboard
  • Linear Mode: Selects text following natural flow (like in terminals)
  • Block Mode: Selects rectangular areas (like in editors)
  • Paste Test: Press Ctrl+V in another app to paste copied text
  • Console Logs: Check browser console for mouse event details
